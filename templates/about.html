This site is powered by d-note, a Free and Open Source Python web application written in Flask. d-note is a self destructing notes application. The idea comes from spy movies, such as Mission Impossible, where the spy will receive his next assignment, shortly after which the message will self-destruct. Message self-destruct after 3 minutes of being viewed. Unread notes will self-destruct 30 days after creation.
